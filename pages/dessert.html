Leadelle's Chocolate Sauce


Ingredients:

1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
1 ½ cup sugar
¾ cup cocoa powder
⅓ cup butter
Pinch of salt
1 tsp vanilla


Directions:

Bring ingredients to a boil and stir for 1 minute. Add vanilla.

Kristine's Breakfast Recipes

Waffles


Ingredients:

2 eggs
1 ¾ cups milk
½ cup butter, melted or vegetable oil
2 cups flour
4 tsp baking powder
1 Tbsp sugar
½ tsp salt


Directions:

Beat eggs with hand beater until fluffy.
Beat in rest of ingredients just until smooth.

Kristine's Dinner Recipes

White Bean Chili


Ingredients:

4 cups dry white beans
1 Tbsp salt
¼ cup dried onion
2 (15 oz) cans yellow hominy, rinsed and drained
1 Tbsp chicken bullion
1 (9 oz) cooked chicken breast strips
½ cup lime juice (~4 limes)
2 Tbsp chopped cilantro/parsley
1 tsp cumin
½ tsp black pepper

Optional toppings:
Bottled green salsa (Hardeez chili/salsa verde is good)
Cheese
Cilantro, chopped
Chips


Directions:

Pressure cook white beans, dried onion, and salt. Add yellow hominy, chicken bullion, chicken breast strips, lime juice, 2 Tbsp chopped cilantro/parsley, 1 tsp cumin, and black pepper. To individual bowls, add optional toppings.

Riley's Beans


Ingredients:

2 cups beans
6 cups water
½ Tbsp cumin
1 Tbsp salt
Some onion powder
Some garlic powder


Directions:

Add beans and water to the pot. Add seasonings. Pressure cook for 50 minutes.
